My Projects

Nobigo:Japanese Language Learning

Nobigo is a Gamified Language Learning app which I had worked on under my 3 months internship peroid at Dark Matter Game Productions.Nobigo is designed to help Nepali learners quickly grasp practical Japanese. It teaches Hiragana, Katakana, and Kanji through interactive tracing, builds essential vocabulary and grammar, and guides users through a structured path suited for studying, working, or daily life in Japan. Features like a built-in Resume Builder also help students and workers prepare for opportunities in Japan.



Shark Attack Simulation

Shark Attack Simulation is a game I had worked on along with my collegue as a part of our academic coursework for the module "Interactive 3D Applications". We can play both as a person and as a shark in the game, while playing as a person our main objective is to reach to the island safely without getting eaten by the shark whereas while playing as the shark our objective is to kill three persons whilst on water to win the game.I handled the Game UI,game logic,QA Testing and various other aspects of the game.
